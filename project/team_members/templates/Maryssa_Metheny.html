Maryssa Metheny

Biography

Maryssa Metheny is a Data Scientist on the Prototyping Team within DST’s Applied Analytics Group. During the three years she’s been with DST, Maryssa has contributed to the development of both client engagements and products, in both the Financial Services and Health Care sectors. Prior to joining DST, Maryssa completed a PhD in Applied Mathematics from Wichita State University and a BS in both Business Administration (emphasis in Finance) and Mathematics from Truman State University. Her dissertation related to work with covariance structures of log-Gaussian stochastic vector processes, which can be used in the study of stock market behaviors. Also, she taught a variety of mathematics and statistics courses during her tenure at WSU. She enjoys employing various mathematical and statistical models to solve both client specific and industry wide problems.
